S. M. Iqbal Mahamud, Ph.D.

Department of Microbiology
University of Dhaka
“My passion for bacteriophage biology drives my mission to combat antimicrobial resistance through computational science and laboratory research.”

S. M. Iqbal Mahamud is a microbiologist and computational biologist specializing in bacteriophage biology, microbial genomics, and bioinformatics. His research focuses on antimicrobial resistance, genomic data analysis, and translational microbiology.

His work integrates wet-lab experimentation with advanced computational methods to better understand microbial pathogenesis and phage-host interactions.
